Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I start a debate with the AI?

To start a debate, simply input your argument in the text box or use the voice recording feature. The AI will then respond with a counterargument.

How do I access the Research tab?

Click on the Research tab in the app, enter your debate topic or country, and the AI will generate relevant information for you to use.

How can I customize the debate settings?

Go to the Settings tab in the app to adjust the country representation and debate topic for both you and the AI opponent.

Is my data secure while using the app?

Yes, your data is secure. Debate Master does not collect or store any personal information or data from its users.

Troubleshooting Tips

The AI is not responding to my arguments. What should I do?

Ensure that you have a stable internet connection, as the AI requires connectivity to function. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

I'm having difficulty with the voice recording feature. How can I resolve this?

Check if your device's microphone is enabled and properly functioning. If the issue continues, try reinstalling the app or contacting our support team for assistance.
